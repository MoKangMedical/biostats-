About BioStatS

An AI-powered biostatistics platform that makes rigorous statistical analysis accessible to every biomedical researcher, from students to principal investigators.

Our Mission

Democratize biostatistics by combining guided workflows with AI-assisted analysis, so researchers can focus on science rather than software.

🎯

Mission Statement

Statistical analysis is the backbone of biomedical research, yet many researchers struggle with choosing the right test, checking assumptions, and interpreting results. BioStatS bridges this gap with guided, AI-assisted workflows that ensure methodological rigor without requiring a statistics PhD.

🔭

Vision

A world where every biomedical study — from a medical student's first research project to a multi-center clinical trial — has access to publication-quality statistical analysis with built-in best practices and reproducibility.

💡

Approach

Combine the power of R and Python statistical ecosystems with an intuitive guided interface. AI recommends the appropriate test, checks assumptions automatically, and generates publication-ready reports — all while maintaining full transparency of methods.

Features Overview

Comprehensive statistical tools designed for medical professionals and biomedical researchers.

📊

50+ Statistical Tests

Guided test selection with automatic assumption checking. From t-tests and ANOVA to non-parametric alternatives, the AI recommends the most appropriate test for your data structure and research question.

📈

Regression Models

Full suite of regression techniques: linear, logistic, Cox proportional hazards, and mixed-effects models. Automated diagnostics including residual analysis, multicollinearity checks, and model fit assessment.

🔄

Sample Size & Power

Interactive power analysis and sample size calculators for study design. Supports a priori, post hoc, and compromise power analysis with effect size estimation from pilot data.

📋

Automated Reporting

One-click generation of publication-ready statistical reports in APA, AMA, and custom formats. Includes tables, figures, assumption checks, and complete method descriptions for the manuscript methods section.

🎯

Survival Analysis

Kaplan-Meier estimation, log-rank tests, and Cox regression with automated survival curve plotting. Support for competing risks analysis and time-varying covariates.

🎨

Interactive Visualization

Publication-quality plots with customizable themes. Forest plots, Bland-Altman plots, ROC curves, and correlation matrices with export to PNG, SVG, and PDF formats.

Technology Stack

Built on proven statistical computing platforms with modern web technologies for accessibility.

🧠

Statistical Engine

R (stats, survival, lme4) and Python (scipy, statsmodels, lifelines) backends. Ensures compatibility with established statistical methods accepted by journals and regulatory agencies worldwide.

🔗

Integration

Compatible with R/Python workflows, CSV/Excel/SPSS data import, export to LaTeX/Word. API access for integration with REDCap, clinical trial management systems, and electronic data capture platforms.

🏗️

Platform

Web-based interface with responsive design. FDA 21 CFR Part 11 compliance pathway for clinical trial analyses. Docker deployment for institutional on-premise installations with full data sovereignty.

Development Roadmap

Evolving from a statistical toolkit to an AI-powered research analytics platform.

✅

Phase 1 — Core Tests (Complete)

50+ statistical tests with guided selection, basic regression models (linear, logistic), sample size calculators, CSV/Excel data import, and initial visualization suite.

🔄

Phase 2 — Advanced Methods (In Progress)

Survival analysis module (Kaplan-Meier, Cox, competing risks), mixed-effects models, automated APA/AMA report generation, and interactive diagnostic plots.

📋

Phase 3 — AI Assistant (Q4 2026)

AI-powered natural language query interface ("compare treatment groups adjusting for age and sex"), automated assumption violation remediation suggestions, and intelligent data cleaning recommendations.

🚀

Phase 4 — Clinical Grade (2027)

FDA 21 CFR Part 11 compliance certification, REDCap and EDC system integration, collaborative multi-analyst workflows, reproducible analysis pipelines, and regulatory submission-ready outputs.
